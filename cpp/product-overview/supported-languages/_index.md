Aspose.OCR for C++ can recognize a large number of languages and all popular writing scripts, including texts with mixed languages.

- Croatian
- Czech
- Danish
- Dutch
- English
- Estonian
- Finnish
- French
- German
- Italian
- Latvian
- Lithuanian
- Norwegian
- Polish
- Portuguese
- Romanian
- Slovak
- Slovenian
- Spanish
- Swedish
